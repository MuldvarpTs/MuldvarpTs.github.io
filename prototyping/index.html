Crazy Cart

  • Max Speed: 20 km/h
  • Battery Time: Up to 40 minutes
  • Age: 9 years and up
  • Max Weight: 60 kg

Drive, spin, or drift like a pro - at home! Drive it like a go-kart or lift the Drift bar to kickstart Crazy Cart mode. With speeds up to 12 mph (19 km/h) and continuous ride time of 40 minutes, everyone can have a ride. Another revolution in driving from Razor! When the Drift bar is down, Crazy Cart drives like a regular go-kart, in the direction of the front wheel. But when the Drift bar is engaged, that's when the real fun begins! Raising the Drift bar changes the angle of the rear caster wheels and allows Crazy Cart to drift forward, backward, sideways, diagonally, and everything in between. Crazy Cart's innovative, patented drive system allows you to drift dynamically and spin infinitely on the spot. Tight turns and even driving in reverse are possible with 360-degree steering capability. There's a global community of Crazy Cart fans who organize meetups, share knowledge online, and showcase their customized vehicles. Follow @CrazyCartOfficial on Instagram and Facebook to stay updated.
